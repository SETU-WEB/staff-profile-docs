# InnerBlockQuote

The **Inner Block Quote** area is used for adding a **block quote**, typically a highlighted statement or testimonial. Be sure to include the **person’s name and title** so readers know who the quote is attributed to. Once you’ve added the quote and details, **don’t forget to save** your changes to ensure everything is properly updated.&#x20;
